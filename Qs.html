Frequently Asked Questions

The service offers three main plans:

  • Server X (Egypt only): 12 months → 350 EGP, 24 months → 650 EGP
  • Basic Plan: 1 year → $35, 2 years → $60
  • Premium Plan: 1 year → $55, 2 years → $100

Yes, Server X offers a 12-hour free trial, while Basic and Premium plans offer a 24-hour free trial. Trial requests must be made via WhatsApp.

The service works on most smart devices: Smart TVs, Android and iOS phones and tablets, Fire TV, Roku, Windows PCs, and Apple TV.

Inside Egypt: Vodafone Cash, CIB Wallet, InstaPay.

Outside Egypt: Payment via TapTap Send and PayPal.

Activation occurs within 5 minutes after payment confirmation (screenshot required). Follow the device-specific installation guide on the official website.

The IBO Player app is highly recommended for stability and performance. IPTV Smarters is not recommended on Samsung and LG devices due to stability issues.

Support is available only via WhatsApp at +201117250227 or through the AI chatbot on the website. No phone calls are supported.
